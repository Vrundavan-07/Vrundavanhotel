About Hotel Vrundavan

Our Story

Founded on 3rd May 2025, Hotel Vrundavan is the dream venture of Mr. Ravi Raut and Mrs. Girija Raut, who envisioned a warm, welcoming place where delicious food meets heartfelt hospitality.

Located in the heart of the city, Hotel Vrundavan offers a perfect blend of traditional flavors and modern ambiance. With attention to detail, a commitment to hygiene, and a passion for taste, we strive to provide every guest with an unforgettable culinary journey.

Whether you're here for a quick bite or a hearty meal, our wide range of offerings caters to every craving and occasion.

  • Snacks: Fresh, crispy, and local favorites
  • Beverages: Hot and cold drinks, juices, shakes, and more
  • Main Course: North Indian, South Indian, and Maharashtrian dishes
  • Thalis: A variety of curated thali meals served with love
